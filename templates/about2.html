Our Leadership Team

Melvin Blaze (Founder and CEO)

Melvin is an accomplished leader with an exceptional academic background. He earned his PhD in Analytical Chemistry with a specialization in Mass Spectrometry from the University of Illinois at Chicago, and he holds an MS in Analytical Chemistry from St. Joseph's College Bangalore (India).

With over 20 years of experience, Melvin has successfully led diverse analytical teams in industries such as Pharmaceuticals (Preclinical, Clinical, and Manufacturing), Food & Beverage, Contract Research Organizations, and Environmental Fate and Metabolism.

During his tenure in the pharmaceutical industry, Melvin spent over 6 years working in the analytical and bioanalytical divisions. He provided crucial support to pre-clinical, clinical, and QC analytical labs at companies including Aurigene Discovery Technologies, a biotechnology firm focused on drug discovery and development, Eurofins Advinus, a contract research organization specializing in regulatory toxicology, CMC services, DMPK, and analytical R&D services, and Medefil, Inc, a reputable medical device and drug manufacturing company.

Melvin also played a key role in building and implementing the metabolomics platform at PepsiCo, Inc, one of the world's leading food and beverage companies. For over 5 years, he oversaw the application of this platform across PepsiCo's major product portfolio, including Tropicana®, Quaker, Pepsi, Frito-Lays, and Gatorade. Additionally, Melvin spent over 4 years at Waters Corporation, a prominent measurement device manufacturing company, where he led the development of novel chromatographic products and automation workflows.

Furthermore, Melvin led a team dedicated to supporting Environmental Fate and Metabolism studies at Smithers one of leading contract research organization for regulated Environmental risk sciences.

With his extensive expertise in analytical chemistry and his proven leadership capabilities, Melvin is a valuable addition to our leadership team. His vast experience across multiple industries positions him to drive success, foster innovation, and contribute to our organization's growth.

Asha Abraham Blaze (Founder and CFO)

Asha is a highly skilled professional with a strong educational background. She holds an MBA in Finance and Marketing from Chinmaya Institute of Technology, India, and a B.Com in Accounting and Finance from the University of Calicut.

With over 9 years of experience, Asha has specialized in credit risk operations and assets & wealth management, consistently surpassing organizational goals in high-pressure environments. Her expertise lies in preparing finance reports, reconciliation reports, country risk reports, past due reports, quality checks, and Key Process Indicators (KPIs).

Asha possesses extensive experience in analyzing and capturing trades within the credit risk management (CRM) central repository. She is proficient in updating static data, maintaining hierarchy, and facilitating the accurate flow of exposure and credit limits.

Previously, Asha worked at TATA Consultancy Services, where she served as the client lead for Deutsche Bank in Singapore. In this role, she successfully managed a team of over 10 associates responsible for different sub-processes, including LS2 Recon, SA RWA (Standard Approach Risk Weighted Assets), CIB unassigned (Corporate & Investment Banking), dbArtos, and Siperian. Asha demonstrated exceptional client management skills, ensuring timely deliverables and addressing any queries with the utmost professionalism. She also generated accurate and timely ad-hoc reports for senior management.

Asha's diverse skill set, coupled with her ability to deliver high-quality results, makes her a valuable asset to our leadership team. Her comprehensive understanding of credit risk operations and wealth management will greatly contribute to our organization's success and enable us to achieve our objectives efficiently.
